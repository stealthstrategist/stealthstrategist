02 / 10 · Situation · Complication · Resolution

Stellarton has the assets. The structure is holding growth back.

Situation

Stellarton holds a #2 market position in premium prepared foods with 22% revenue growth over 3 years — yet EBITDA has compressed from 18% to 11% in the same period.

Complication

Three structural inefficiencies: SKU proliferation (487 SKUs, 38% generating under 1% of revenue), fragmented distribution (6 overlapping logistics partners), and a 22-month product development cycle.

Resolution

Focused structural change can restore 600–800 basis points of margin. A three-phase transformation can return Stellarton to 17–19% EBITDA within 18 months without sacrificing revenue momentum.

04 / 10 · Strategic Recommendations

Three moves. Eighteen months. 600 basis points of margin.

01

SKU Rationalization & Portfolio Discipline

Discontinue 142 underperforming SKUs, restructure around 80 core contributors, establish margin-gating process. Free $12M in working capital.

+220–280 bps · 90 days
02

Distribution Network Consolidation

Consolidate from 6 logistics partners to 2 strategic partners. Renegotiate with volume-based pricing leverage. Improve OTIF to 96%+.

+180–220 bps · 6 months
03

Product Development Acceleration

Compress 22-month cycle to 9 months through stage-gate redesign, co-manufacturer pre-qualification, and consumer testing overhaul.

+200–300 bps revenue · 12 months

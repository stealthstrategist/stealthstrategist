The problem

Snowflake credits burn silently.

Most startups discover their Snowflake cost problem when the bill lands — not before. By then, the waste has been compounding for months.

Waste #1
Warehouses running 24/7 with no timeout
Auto-resume with no auto-suspend means warehouses spin up for a single query and idle for hours burning credits.
Waste #2
Oversized warehouses for lightweight queries
X-LARGE warehouses running dbt tests or dashboard refreshes that a SMALL could handle for 1/16th the cost.
Waste #3
No resource monitors or spend alerts
Without monitors, a runaway query or misconfigured job can burn a month's credit budget overnight with no warning.
Waste #4
Full table scans on unpartitioned data
Queries hitting millions of rows without clustering keys or search optimization force expensive full scans every run.

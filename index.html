How it works

Four steps. No
surprises.

01

Brief & Discovery

You share your content, brand assets, and the goal of the deck. 30-min kickoff call or async brief. I ask the questions that shape the whole project before a single slide is touched.

02

Narrative Strategy

I map the story arc and slide outline before any design begins. Structure is 80% of the work. The design only lands when the narrative already does.

03

Design & Build

Full design in PowerPoint or Google Slides — branded, polished, and built to your audience. First draft in 48–72 hrs. Every element earns its place.

04

Revise & Deliver

Two rounds of revisions included. Final file delivered in fully editable format plus PDF export — so you can update it yourself after delivery without starting over.
